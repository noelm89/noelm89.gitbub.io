Activities

  • Ski/Boarding

    Washington State offers a variety of ski/board locations to choose from. This includes Crystal Mountain, Mt. Baker, Snoqualmie Pass and Steven's Pass.

  • Hiking

    Even though it pours rain in Seattle, a great exchange for the drizzle is beautiful scenery at many different hiking trails. Try places such as Poo Poo Point, Mail Box Peak and Lake Serene.
