Restaurants/Bars

  • Buca di Beppo ​

    If you like Italian cuisine this is the place for you. The restaurant has a cute vintage style and the food is big family style servings.

    ​

  • Tango

    Tango's menu has Spanish inspired dishes incuding tapas, paella and sangria! They cook the paella to order so be prepared to wait 45 minutes for it to cook. The result is delicious!

  • Taco Chuki

    Mexican

  • Nijo

    located near Pike Place Market makes it the perfect pit stop to refuel on sushi after shopping. Has a great menu of fresh cut sushi and an extensive list of sake

​

Activities

    ​

  • Skiing/Boarding

    image credit: here

    There are so many places to ski and board just a short drive from Seattle. Ski fields like Summit at Snoqualamie and Crystal mountain are frequented by locals. You'll learn to love the rain in the winter because it means a fresh layer of powder up in the mountains!

    ​

  • Hiking

    image credit: here

    When the snow melts there are so many national and state parks to explore. Burn off the week of work by climbing Poo Poo point or Mailbox peak. Alpine lakes are also a plently with walks to lake Serene or Lake 22 a great way to spend a sunny day.
